About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my Portfolio page. I would say my journey into this field of tech started when I enrolled in school to study Computer Engineering.

However, I actually properly started being interested in my third year in school. I liked the concept of code, it just looked cool to me and so I started asking questions.

With time, I noticed there was a whole community of people learning and working together. It was wonderful to see how so many people were sitting together to help each other grow.

This inspired me to actually start learning it and all of this happened before I even knew that there was money in tech.

My journey in tech has not stopped since it began and there has been alot of struggles but in all, I like it and am willing to keep putting in effort to being excellent in my field. Overall, I love the fact that this has brought me closer to an awesome bunch of people.
